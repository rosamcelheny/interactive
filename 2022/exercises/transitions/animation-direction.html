In this example, each circle has a different animation-direction. These circles have the same animation applied, turning the color from yellow to purple. The animation plays three times through.

animation-direction: forwards means the animation plays forwards each time.

animation-direction: forwards

animation-direction: reverse means the animation plays backwards each time.

animation-direction: reverse

animation-direction: alternate means the animation plays forwards first, and then backwards.

animation-direction: alternate
