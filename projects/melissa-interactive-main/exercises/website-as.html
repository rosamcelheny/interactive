WEBSITE AS TRAVEL

Websites are a walk. We meander from one web destination to another at a slow and sometimes nonlinear pace, stopping to admire things along the way.

Websites are a drive. We cruise through the web with a destination in mind, glancing out of the window at other destinations passing by.

Websites are a boat ride. We float above the surface with an entire ecosystem of information waiting to be found just below.

Websites are a plane ride. We quickly travel between two completely different destinations.

Websites are teleportation. We jump between sites at a rapid pace, following new ideas and information spontaneously as they arise.
